* CRICKET *

About the Cricket

Cricket is a team sport that involves batting, fielding, and bowling, and is played between two teams of 11 players on an oval-shaped field

Origine

Cricket originated in England and is now popular in many countries, including India, Pakistan, Australia, and South Africa. The first written reference to cricket dates back to the end of the 16th century




Rules of Cricket

  1. The pitch is a rectangular area in the middle of the field where the bowler delivers the ball to the batsman. It is 22 yards long and 10 feet wide
  2. The crease is the line that marks the batsman's safe zone. It consists of the popping crease and the batting crease.
  3. Batsmen score runs by striking the ball and running between the wickets.
  4. The ball is considered dead when the fielding side and both batters at the wicket have stopped regarding it as in play.
  5. Test cricket is the longest and purest form of the game, spanning over five days.
  6. Cricket is played between two teams with eleven players on each side.
