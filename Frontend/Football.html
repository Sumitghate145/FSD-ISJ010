* FOOTBALL *

About Football

Football is a popular sport that involves two teams competing to score more points than the other by moving a ball into their opponent's goal

Players

Each team has 11 players, with 10 outfield players and one goalkeeper. The goalkeeper can use their hands within the penalty area.

Ball

The ball is usually brown and oval-shaped, but the shape varies by type of football. For example, the ball used in association football is spherical, while the ball used in American football is shaped like a lemon.



Football Rules

  1. Each team has 11 players, including one goalie. Players can only touch the ball with their legs or torso, except for goalkeepers.
  2. The field is split into two halves by a line parallel to the goal line. The touchlines are 90–120 meters long, and the goal lines are 45–90 meters wide.
  3. A match is made up of two 45-minute halves. The referee and teams can agree to reduce the length of the halves before the match.
  4. The team that scores the most goals wins.
  5. A player is offside if they are on the wrong side of the line of scrimmage when the snap happens.
  6. The official international soccer federation, FIFA, authorizes soccer regulations in a 130-page rule book.
  7. A foul is when a player breaks the rules, such as causing another player to trip.
