About Hockey

History:

The origins of hockey can be traced back to ancient times, with references found in Iran, Egypt, and Greece. The first rules of modern hockey were established in the United Kingdom in 1876. Hockey first appeared in the Olympics in 1908

Types of Hockey

There are multiple types of hockey, including field hockey and ice hockey:

  • Field hockey: A popular outdoor game played on grass or turf, with two teams of 11 players. The goal is to push a heavy ball into the other team's goal.
  • Ice hockey: The PCHA introduced many changes to the game, including dividing the ice into three zones and allowing forward passing in the center zone.
